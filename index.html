The Pro Wrestling League is the initiative of ProSportify owned by Kartikeya Sharma and Wrestling Federation of India, to be held in between 10–27 December 2015 in India.The first season of this league will be among 6 city-based teams, with 66 wrestlers from different countries of the world.Three-time women’s world champion Adeline Gray of USA called the league a “historic move in international wrestling that, by bringing in huge sums of money, has taken a bold step in popularizing the sport at world level.

There will be six teams, each consisting of a total of 1 Icon and 8 other players. Among them, there will be 5 Indians and 4 foreign players, consisting of 5 men and 4 women. Out of these 5 foreign players, only 4 can be part of a tie.[2] Each tie will be contested based on 7 best bouts. A total of 18 ties (15 league ties) will be played.The league will be played on the 'best of nine'.

The bidding took place on 3 November, where 6 franchises participated. 159 wrestlers participated in the bid, and 101 wrestlers remained unsold.[4] each franchise had a purchasing cap of Rs 2 crore. One Icon Player was stamped at a base price of Rs 33 lakh. Ukraine's Oksana Herhel went for the highest bid of the day, and she was bought or Rs 41.3 lakh by the Team Haryana
